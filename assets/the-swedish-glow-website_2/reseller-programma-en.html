Questions upfront

Frequently asked questions.

What are the minimum order requirements?
We operate without fixed minimum monthly orders. However, we do require an initial introductory order so that you can present the complete collection in your practice. The exact terms are discussed during the introductory meeting — tailored to the size and focus of your business.
Do I receive exclusivity in my region?
For certain categories of professionals — such as plastic surgeons and skin therapists — we offer regional exclusivity. For other partners we apply a careful distribution policy so that partners complement rather than compete with each other.
What marketing support do I receive?
You gain access to an extensive library of professional product photography, social media templates, videos, flyers and in-store materials. We also offer bespoke content on request for your specific channels and target audience.
How does the ordering process work?
Through our reseller portal you have 24/7 access to stock levels, pricing and your order history. Orders placed before 14:00 are dispatched the same day. Invoicing is handled automatically on a monthly basis.
Do I receive training on the products?
Yes, all new partners are invited to a comprehensive product onboarding. We also organise periodic webinars, ingredient deep-dives and an annual partner event where we share the latest developments.
What if a product does not suit my client?
We operate a flexible returns policy for partners. Unsold products in their original packaging may be returned under certain conditions, and if in doubt we are happy to advise you personally on which product best suits your clientele.
