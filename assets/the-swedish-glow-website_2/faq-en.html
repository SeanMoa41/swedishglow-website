About our supplements

Liquid collagen is preferred by many because it is immediately absorbable and can contain a higher dose per serving. Our Marine Collagen delivers 13,000 mg per daily 20 ml shot — one of the highest doses on the European market. What works best varies per person, but consistent intake is always the most important factor.

Clinical research shows effects on skin firmness and hydration from 2,500 to 10,000 mg of collagen per day. Our formula with 13,000 mg per shot is well above the recommended lower limit, so you won't risk under-dosing. Results are typically visible between 6 and 12 weeks with daily use.

Marine collagen (from fish) consists primarily of type I collagen, the type most prevalent in skin. Bovine collagen contains type I as well as a large amount of type III, which is particularly interesting for joints. Marine collagen is often absorbed more quickly due to its smaller peptide size. We deliberately chose marine collagen for its focus on skin, hair and nails.

FREJA is our new flagship Omega 3 — a premium liquid formula from sustainable North Sea fisheries with a high concentration of EPA and DHA.
